DESCRIPTION

  • Bring life back into your keyboard by bringing tons of useful functionalities to the unused keyboard space. Shortmoji comes packed with tons of options to customize Keyboard, Predictive Bar and Keyboard Dock.

  • Key Features:
    • Adds Emojis bar into Predictive Bar/Bottom Bar with support of Skin Tones.
    • Adds Pasteboard Shortcuts bar into Predictive Bar/Bottom Bar.
    • Keyboard customization (set font size, Keyboard color, Keyboard height.. etc).
    • Change Keyboard Key Text Color to Custom Color or to Razer Chroma RGB.
    • Change Keyboard Color to Light, Dark, OLED Black, Full OLED Black or to a Custom Color.
    • Hide Keyboard Key Shadows.
    • Hide Keyboard Key Caps.
    • Globe/Emoji button customization (with Double-tap and Long Press actions).
    • Dictation button customization (with Double-tap and Long Press actions).
    • Home Bar customization (set width, height.. etc).
    • Enable iPhone X keyboard Layout fon non-notched devices.

    Available Shortcuts:
    Copy, Cut, Paste, Select, Select All, Delete, Undo, Redo, Capitalized Case, Uppercase, Lowercase, Dismiss keyboard, CopyLog, Define, Beginning of Line, Ending of Line, Beginning of Document, Ending of Document, Cursor Down, Cursor Up, Cursor Left, Cursor Right, Previous Word, Next Word, Dictation, Language, Language (without Emoji), Emojis, Wasabi, Translomatic, Pasithea, Left Handed Keyboard and Right Handed Keyboard.
